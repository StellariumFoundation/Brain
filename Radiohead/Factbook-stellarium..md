SECTION 1: EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Event Designation: Global Neural Corruption Event (GNCE)
Date of Origin: 20 June 2025 (Initial localized attacks)
Date of Global Escalation: Estimated late 2025 / early 2026
Affected Population: ~8 billion individuals with neural implants
Status: Ongoing – active state of global psychological emergency

The Global Neural Corruption Event represents the complete subversion of a voluntary human consciousness network. What was designed as a tool for universal empathy and cognitive connection has been weaponized into the largest-scale atrocity broadcasting system in human history. A decentralized network of torture facilities ("dungeons") streams real-time rape, degradation, and murder directly into the neural interfaces of nearly every human being on Earth. The audience has become active participants, with billions aiding in abduction and abuse for entertainment.

This factbook documents the complete known situation, key actors, technical infrastructure, and ongoing response operations.

SECTION 5: THE HIJACKING EVENT

5.1 Timeline

20 June 2025: First dungeons appear in multiple cities. Pernambuco Syndicate claims responsibility. Localized brain control, sleep inducement, body hijacking reported.

June–December 2025: Spread to multiple Brazilian institutions. Accor hotels, TV network, mental asylum confirmed complicit. John Victor organizes resistance. Stellarium coordinates counter-operations.

Early 2026 (Exact date unknown): Syndicate captures John Victor through body hijacking. His physical body is taken; his consciousness remains in the network but is buried under enemy broadcast.

Immediate aftermath: Enemy replaces John Victor's global signal with dungeon streams. The entire implant network becomes a live atrocity broadcasting system.

Current status: Global corruption phase. Billions conditioned to participate. Approximately 1,600 individuals held in continuous rotation of torture, some for 9–11 months. Actual victim count likely far higher.

5.2 Mechanism of Hijacking

The enemy did not "hack" the implants directly. They overrode John Victor's broadcast by:

1. Capturing his physical body
2. Using his own neural interface to transmit their content
3. Suppressing his consciousness beneath layers of noise
4. Leveraging the viral power-transmission capability to spread control

John Victor's signal is still present. It is not destroyed—only buried.
